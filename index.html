Our corner

A proper corner caff

B&D's Corner Café is a friendly, family-run spot on Coast Road in Blackhall Colliery — generous breakfasts, homemade chips, and always a warm welcome (and a bowl of water for the dog).

Everything's cooked fresh: full breakfasts from a Small right up to the mighty Belly Buster, burgers and crispy chicken wraps with those famous homemade chips, cold sandwiches, kids' meals, sweet treats and afternoon tea for two. Dine in, grab a takeaway, or order on Just Eat and we'll bring it to your door.
